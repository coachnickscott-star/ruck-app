Privacy Policy

Ruck — Rugby Match Tracker · Last updated: 5 April 2026

What we collect

When you first open Ruck, we ask for your email address. This is stored locally on your device and used solely to contact you about product updates. We do not sell, share, or transfer your email to any third party.

Data stored on your device

All match data, team information, squad sheets, and match history are stored locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage). This data never leaves your device. We do not have access to it and cannot retrieve it.

Analytics and advertising

Ruck may display advertisements via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's privacy policy. We do not collect any analytics data ourselves.

Third-party services

When you use the Share feature, data is passed to the platform you choose to share to (such as WhatsApp, Instagram, or X). We do not control how those platforms handle shared content.

Children's privacy

Ruck is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
