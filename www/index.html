Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a securely hashed version of your password. We do not store your password in plain text. Match data (teams, scores, events, timelines) is stored locally on your device.

How we use it

Your email address is used to identify your account and, in future, to notify you about product updates and new features. We will never sell your data or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

Where it's stored

Account data is stored securely on Supabase servers located in the EU (West Europe, London). Match data remains on your device and is not uploaded to any server.

Your rights

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing us. Upon request, all personal data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

Contact

For any privacy questions or deletion requests, contact us at hello@rugbycoach.ai
